Brian Dumaine

Senior Editor at Large, Fortune Magazine
Interviewer, Closing Keynote

Brian Dumaine is a senior editor at large at FORTUNE, covering international business and green technology and environmental policy stories. He is the author of the The Plot To Save The Planet: How Visionary Entrepreneurs and Corporate Titans Are Creating Real Solutions To Global Warming.

Dumaine has worked at FORTUNE for 30 years in various writing and editing positions including global editor and assistant managing editor. He has won numerous journalism awards and written more than 100 feature stories for the magazine, including covers such as "America’s Toughest Bosses," "The Innovation Gap," and "America’s Smartest Young Entrepreneurs." Throughout his career, he has produced investigative pieces as well as articles on marketing, investing, technology, and corporate crime.

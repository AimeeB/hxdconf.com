Zoe Finch Totten

Founder and CEO of The Full Yield, Inc.
Bio

Zoe Finch Totten is Founder and CEO of The Full Yield, Inc. For the past 20 years, Zoe has been developing and refining her vision for The Full Yield Inc., whose first program was successfully piloted with Harvard Pilgrim Health Care, Roche Bros. Supermarkets, two national food manufacturers, three food service vendors, and seven Boston-area employers between February 2010 and August 2011. Now available nationally direct to employer, direct to consumer, and through select health plans, this comprehensive program for individuals exists within strategically and operationally integrated collaborations between the healthcare and food industries.

Formerly a Yale-trained nurse-midwife and a food industry consultant, Zoe is an Associate at the Jefferson School of Population Health at Thomas Jefferson University and a member of the Private and Public, Scientific, Academic, and Consumer Food Policy Group at Harvard Business School. Using cross-industry knowledge and an historic base in clinical and nutrition care, she has fostered partnerships to create market-disruptive employer-purchasing groups within the food industry and retailer-based healthy foods scoring programs.
